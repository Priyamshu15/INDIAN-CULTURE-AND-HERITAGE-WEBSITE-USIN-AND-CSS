Cities and Cultural Heritage

Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Jama Masjid.

Mumbai

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a melting pot of cultures. It is famous for its Bollywood film industry and historical sites like the Gateway of India.

Kolkata

Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, is known for its literary heritage and artistic traditions. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Victoria Memorial and Howrah Bridge.

Chennai

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, is known for its rich history and classical music and dance forms. It is home to famous temples like the Kapaleeshwarar Temple.

Bangalore

Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India, is known for its vibrant IT industry and pleasant climate. It is home to beautiful gardens and historical sites like the Bangalore Palace.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the city of pearls, is known for its rich history and architectural marvels like the Charminar and Golconda Fort. It is also famous for its delicious biryani.

Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City of India, is known for its magnificent palaces and forts. It is home to iconic landmarks like the Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort.

Kashmir

Kashmir is dotted with a large number of heritage sites that include not only palaces and gardens but also Buddhist ruins, mosques as well as Hindu Temples. Amongst the top heritage attractions in Kashmir region is Pari Mahal, also known as the Abode of Fairies.
